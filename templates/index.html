Keeping things simple.

The idea for "The Marksheet Project" came up when our faculty at SIES GST told us about various problems they were facing when it came to student results entry and analysis.

The problems were:
1. Results came in PDF format and then had to be manually entered by the faculty into Excel. This was time consuming and error-prone.
2. Analysis was the second major task, where teachers would have to go through the data and create tables, charts/visualisations, etc.

And that's it, that's what got us thinking and we started "The Marksheet Project"
